Questions, answered

The things couples usually ask.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend 3–4 months ahead for weekend weddings, especially April–October. Weekday weddings can often be accommodated with 6 weeks' notice.

Can we upload our own artwork?

Absolutely. You can use a template as-is, personalise one with your details, or send us your own illustration or photo — we'll optimise it for print.

Does the machine actually take coins?

It does — but we load it with complimentary tokens so guests don't need to fumble for change. A small bowl of tokens sits next to the machine with custom signage.

What space does the machine need?

About 1m × 1m of floor space, near a power outlet. We can also run on battery if your venue is outdoors.

What if we run out of stickers?

Our packages are sized generously for your guest count and typically end the night with plenty left. Whatever remains is boxed up and sent home with you.

Do you travel beyond the city?

Yes — we travel island-wide and to select destination weddings. Travel is quoted separately based on distance and setup complexity.
